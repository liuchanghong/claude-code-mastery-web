Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Claude Code for Coders ("the Site") handles your information when you visit https://liuchanghong.github.io/claude-code-mastery-web/.

1. Information We Collect

The Site is a static website hosted on GitHub Pages. We do not operate any servers or databases, and we do not directly collect personal information.

The following third-party services may collect data automatically:

  • GitHub Pages — hosts this site and may log standard server access data (IP address, browser type, pages visited) per GitHub's Privacy Statement.
  • Google Fonts — loads typefaces from Google's servers. Google may collect usage data per their Privacy Policy.
  • Google AdSense (if enabled) — serves advertisements and uses cookies to personalize ads based on your browsing activity. You can opt out via Google Ad Settings.
  • Google Analytics (if enabled) — collects anonymized usage statistics such as page views and session duration.

2. Cookies

The Site itself uses localStorage (not cookies) to remember your language preference (English or Chinese) and theme (light or dark). This data never leaves your browser and is not transmitted to any server.

Third-party services (Google AdSense, Google Analytics) may set cookies in your browser. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings.

3. How We Use Information

We use aggregated, anonymized analytics data solely to understand how readers use the Site and to improve its content and user experience. We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties.

4. Third-Party Links

The Site contains links to external websites (e.g., GitHub, Apple Developer Documentation). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites.

5. Children's Privacy

The Site is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under applicable privacy laws (such as GDPR or CCPA) to access, correct, or delete personal data. Since we do not directly collect personal data, most requests should be directed to the relevant third-party services listed above.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

8. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact the site author via GitHub.
